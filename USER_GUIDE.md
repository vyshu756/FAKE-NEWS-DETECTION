## 📖 How to Use the Application

### Step 1: Open the Application

- Click the link provided to you
- The application works on:
  - 💻 Desktop computers
  - 📱 Mobile phones
  - 📱 Tablets
  - Any device with a web browser

### Step 2: Analyze News Article

1. **Copy a news article** from any source
2. **Paste it** into the text area
3. **Click "Analyze Article"** button
4. **Wait 2-3 seconds** for AI analysis

### Step 3: View Results

You'll see:
- ✅ **Prediction:** "Real News" or "Fake News"
- 📊 **Confidence Score:** How confident the AI is (0-100%)
- 🎯 **Credibility Score:** Overall credibility rating
- 🔑 **Important Keywords:** Words that influenced the decision

### Step 4: Check Analytics (Optional)

- Click "Dashboard" tab
- View statistics:
  - Total predictions made
  - Percentage of fake vs real news
  - Distribution charts

## 🎓 Tips for Best Results

### ✅ DO:
- Paste complete articles (at least 2-3 sentences)
- Include headlines and main content
- Use clear, readable text
- Test multiple sources

### ❌ DON'T:
- Submit single words or very short text
- Include only URLs (paste the actual article text)
- Submit non-English text
- Expect 100% accuracy (use as a tool, not absolute truth)

## 🔍 Understanding Results

### Confidence Score:
- **90-100%:** Very confident
- **70-89%:** Moderately confident
- **50-69%:** Low confidence
- **Below 50%:** Uncertain

### Important Keywords:
- Words highlighted are those that most influenced the AI's decision
- Look for sensational language, emotional triggers, or credible sources

### Credibility Score:
- **80-100:** Highly credible
- **60-79:** Moderately credible
- **40-59:** Questionable
- **0-39:** Low credibility
